Who we are

JC is a comprehensive factory in China, our main job is to produce metal, plastic as raw materials for accessories for shoes, clothes, jewelry.

We have direct stores and offices in Yiwu and Wenzhou. Wenzhou and Lishui have their own factories. We have been engaged in manufacturing for 20 years. Our products have been sold to India, Pakistan, Turkey, Brazil, Mexico and many other countries. We have served many professional customers.
